What is Emotional Intelligence? EQ is the capacity to comprehend, make constructive use of, and regulate one's own emotions in order to reduce stress, communicate clearly, sympathise with others, overcome obstacles, and prevent conflict.


How is it different from IQ? Your IQ measures how well you can reason, comprehend, and convey complicated concepts. EQ is the capacity to identify emotions in both oneself and others. This knowledge can help you make wise decisions that will not only be good for you but also for those around you.


Why is it important? Success in life is determined by many factors, including EQ, in addition to IQ. It's crucial to be able to recognise and control your emotions. In order to establish relationships, it will also assist you in comprehending the feelings of others. Employers today look for soft talents as well as technical ones, such as the capacity to function well in a team. This will lessen team tension, prevent conflicts, and boost overall job satisfaction.
