What is the difference between Margin, Border, and Padding? In CSS, padding is the area between an element's border and its content, whereas a margin is the area surrounding an element's border. To put it another way, the padding property controls the interior space of an element, and the margin property controls the area around it.

What is Emotional Intelligence? Emotional Intelligence, or Emotional Quotient, is defined as one’s ability to identify, understand and handle one’s own emotions as well as perceive what others are feeling.

How is it different to IQ? A person's intelligence level is determined by how well they can solve tasks in a single or series of standardised exams. EQ, on the other hand, is the capacity to recognise, assess, and understand one's own and other people's emotions.

Why is it important? Success in life is determined by many factors, including EQ, in addition to IQ. It's crucial to be able to recognise and control your emotions. In order to establish relationships, it will also assist you in comprehending the feelings of others. Employers today look for soft talents as well as technical ones, such as the capacity to function well in a team. This will lessen team tension, prevent conflicts, and boost overall job satisfaction.
